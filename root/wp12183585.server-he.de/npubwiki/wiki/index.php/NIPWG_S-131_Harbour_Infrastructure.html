The Hrbr Infrstrctr Description as provided to HSSC.

An Excel spreadsheet based on the scope discussion SNPWG had back in the year 2005 Hrbr Infrstrctr Scope. This information is enhanced with advice on other international bodies activities, such as IMO FAL or PortCDM.

Additional reference materials are the latest version of the Port Information Manual (3.03 only nautical data) and Port Information Manual (3.02 Core document), issued by the International Harbour Master Association (IHMA), and the comments provided by NIPWG7 on this Manual. It shoud be noted that the IHMA is working on a revision of the Manual taking into account the NIPWG7 comments. The IHMA Response paper can be found here IHMA Response on NIPWG comments on PIM version 007


A clean Excel spreadsheet where your contributions and comments will be collected. It is intended to keep this spreadsheet up-to-date to ensure that always the current status is available. The first entries have been made in this spreadsheet as an example. Hrbr Infrstrctr Clean
