raphael (talk) 03:53, 2 February 2017 (CET): Revision to Remarks section, to conform to replacement of TXTDSC by (new) information complex attribute and avoid use of 6-letter acronyms. Also, there is no 'Conditions' section in the registry.
Remarks:

  • Exactly one of sub-attributes onlineResource or information must be completed in one instance of textContent.
  • Product specifications may restrict the use or content of onlineResource for security. For example, a product specification may forbid populating onlineResource.
  • Product specification authors must consider whether applications using the data product may be prevented from accessing off-system resources by security policies.

Also needs a definition.
Proposed definition: Textual material, or a pointer to a resource providing textual material. May be accompanied by basic information about its source and relationship to the source.
Remarks:

  • textContent is intended to allow text passages from other publications to be included in, or referenced by, instances of feature or information types.
  • Textual information for which no appropriate specialized Text-type attribute is bound to the feature/information type can be encoded using either information or textContent complex attributes. Generally, either information or textContent is allowed, but not both.
