NIPWG S-123 marine radio services

From IHO Nautical Information Processing Working Group
Jump to navigation Jump to search

This page is the work record of the NIPWG sub-working group for defining updates to S-123 (Marine Radio Services) Edition 1.0.0. It contains the collected feedback received on Edition 1.0.0 and the sub-WG's decisions about updates. Discussions about particular issues should take place on the discussion page (click the discussion tab at the top).

Feedback on S-123 Marine Radio Services Edition 1.0.0


The following documents contain feedback received on S-123 1.0.0.

  • Cumulative list of comments received as of 2022-01-14: Comments list as of 2022-01-14 (.docx file)
  • Proposal to add Tides and Water Flow Forecasting Services: NIPWG8-49.4 (now merged into cumulative list)
  • Report on Creation of Canadian S-123 datasets: NIPWG VTC 2021-6.2.1, NIPWG8-49.2 (now merged into the cumulative list)
  • Maritime Assistance Services and Rescue Services: NIPWG VTC 2021-6.2.2 (now merged into the cumulative list)
  • Report on creation of S-123 for German waters: NIPWG7-20.1b (now merged into the cumulative list)

New feedback should be submitted to Elena Armanino or Bridget Gagné using the comments form (see the Cumulative list... link above). Addresses can be obtained from the NIPWG membership list on the IHO NIPWG page.
